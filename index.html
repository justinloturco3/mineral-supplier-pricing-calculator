How tariffs are modeled. Defaults reflect US import policy under the June 2026 Section 232 proclamation: 50% on semi-finished copper (wire, rod, tube, sheet) and 25% on derivatives (cable, connectors), applied to full customs value; refined cathode / ore is currently exempt pending the Commerce review due 30 Jun 2026. Preferential partners (EU, UK, Japan, Korea) are capped near 15%; USMCA goods from Canada/Mexico carry a ~15% effective floor on non-US content. If your country of operations is not the US, treat tariff cells as editable placeholders. Every figure here is an editable estimate, not a quote.
